Frequently Asked Questions

What services does JAPAL Property Management offer?
We specialize in property management, sales and leasing, valuation & advisory, letting services, and real estate consultancy.
Where are your offices located?
Our headquarters is located in Machakos Town at the Sartaj Building, and we have regional branches in Nairobi, Malindi, and Mombasa.
Why should I choose JAPAL to manage my property?
Established in 2001, we bring over two decades of trusted experience to the table. We utilize technology-driven operations, including cloud-based property management systems, to provide highly professional and personalized service.
Do you handle both residential and commercial properties?
Yes, our clientele includes individual property owners, developers, and corporate organizations. We expertly handle everything from residential apartments and villas to large retail malls and office complexes.

A Premier Real Estate Firm

Japal Property Management Company Limited (JAPMET) is a premier real estate management and consultancy firm established in 2001. With over two decades of experience, the company has consistently delivered professional, reliable, and value-driven services to property owners, investors, institutions, and residents across Kenya.

Headquartered in Machakos Town, we have expanded our footprint to Nairobi, Malindi, and Mombasa, positioning ourselves as a trusted partner locally and regionally.

Our Mission

To shape the future of real estate by creating rewarding opportunities and exceptional spaces for occupiers, businesses, and communities while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, expertise, and integrity.

Our Vision

To be a leading global real estate services firm delivering exceptional value through innovative, technology-driven, and sustainable solutions.
